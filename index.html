About me

My name is Stephanie Rusyaeva

I am an artist currently based in Dubai, UAE. My paintings reached many homes and private collections of people from around the world including USA, Canada, Russia, UK, UAE, Egypt and many more.

I am constantly evolving my skills in order to offer more complicated and professional paintings. In my art I like to mix up realistic and abstract styles, to transforms my feelings into colour and my emotions into contrasts. I favor the use of bright and opposing colours in the composition which I create using oils on canvas.

Even though I am a full time artist, I has never attended a professional art school or acquired any art degree, however I always been interested in drawing from my childhood. Life gave me another profession and after working in a lot of different companies, I realized that only working on art truly fulfilled me. After moving to Dubai, UAE, I started to attend art courses and tutoring courses online from all over the world, which has led me to being a full time artist.

I love to travel and I travel a lot: the beauty of nature, different places, people, all these inspiring me and leave marks in my art. Every time I paint a new artwork, I create a new little universe with its unique vibes. I fill my paintings with love which I am ready to share with others.
